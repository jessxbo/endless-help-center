What is the Endless Studios platform?

The Endless platform is free to access and open to everyone.

Do I need coding experience to participate?

Not at all. Our Explore programs are built for beginners, with no prior coding or game-making experience required. You will learn by doing: modifying real games, experimenting with tools, and building things step by step with support from guides and mentors.

If you want to go deeper into code down the line, Explore: Godot introduces GDScript in a hands-on way. But you can participate fully from day one without writing a single line of code.

Why is the platform free?

Open source and open licenses are core to our mission.
